Eagle Lake to Beddington Lake

Introduction

  The portion of the Narraguagus River from Beddington Lake to roughly 9-km upstream has several restoration projects in the works.
As the design engineer for these projects, I've already made several sites visits to this portion of river. Although I hope to get a site visit in
this spring (the river is a 3+ hour drive one way), ice-out isn't expected till sometime in April and the landowner typically closes the access roads
during mud season, so this season's field work on the Narraguagus may not start till this course is over with. I'll be using photos from the mentioned
sites vists, survey data and LiDAR data in my analysis.
