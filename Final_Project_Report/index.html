Introduction

Quick Summarry:  - Laterally unconfined river segment: see "The Oxbow".
 - Laterally partly confined river segments: see the "Single channel with wooded floodplain" reach in
   the "Route 9 to Beddington Lake" area of interest.  - Laterally confined river segment: There are no long streches of highly confined river in the upper Narraguagus,
   there are several short streches where the river is confined, see the "Ledge Outcrop" reach in the
   "Route 9 to Beddington Lake" area of interest.

  In the future, this web site will hopefully be used to introduce the reader to the upper portion of the Narraguagus River.
The Narraguagus river is one of Maine's coastal Atlantic salmon rivers and is actively being restored. For more information
related to these restoration efforts see the following:
