Sub-Area 2: S-Bend


Sub-Area_2 consits of 4 reaches, the "Riffle" reach is adjacent to wetland on river-right and an esker enlargement on
river-left. The reach "Run with Shallow Pools" runs between the same esker enlargment and an esker on river-right.
